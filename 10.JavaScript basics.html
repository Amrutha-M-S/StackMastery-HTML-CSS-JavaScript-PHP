PET ANIMALS

Dogs

One of the reasons why dogs make good pets is because they help with loneliness.
There are times in life where we are not surrounded by lots of friends and family members.
During these times, having a dog as a companion can be very comforting.
Dogs love being around their owners and love to please them.

Cats

Cats are easy to care for Unlike dogs, cats do not require walk.
Their care is fairly minimal.
Health benefits Pet owners have reduced levels of stress.

Birds

A pet bird is friendly, gentle, and well-suited to being a companion.
These traits are important for people with limited pet bird experience.
It's easier to bond with and care for an animal that has a naturally friendly disposition,
rather than one that tends to be timid.
